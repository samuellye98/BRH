Who We Are

Thousands of Cornell Students every year struggle to buy and sell items, reliant upon a Facebook page which does not update or track the item available, does not cleanly link the buyer and the seller, and is extremely difficult to trawl through. Corsell's goal is to create a dedicated online marketplace for second-hand textbooks, furniture, lifestyle items, educational materials, and subletting of houses.

Through the creation of a dedicated space, we hope to make items accessible to any person who might need them, and ease the interactions between buyers looking to buy, and sellers looking to sell.

Any person, any product - all on Corsell.
